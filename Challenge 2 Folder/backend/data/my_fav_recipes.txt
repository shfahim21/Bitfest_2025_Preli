Name: Chocolate Cake
Ingredients:
- 2 cups flour
- 1.5 cups sugar
- 3/4 cup cocoa powder
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup milk
Instructions:
1. Mix dry ingredients
2. Add wet ingredients
3. Bake at 180°C for 30 minutes
Taste: Sweet
Cuisine: Dessert
Prep Time: 60
Reviews:
- "Perfect for birthdays!"
- "Rich and moist"

Name: Spaghetti Aglio e Olio
Ingredients:
- 200g spaghetti
- 4 cloves garlic
- 1/2 cup olive oil
- Red pepper flakes
Instructions:
1. Cook pasta al dente
2. Sauté garlic in oil
3. Combine and serve
Taste: Savory
Cuisine: Italian
Prep Time: 20
Reviews:
- "Simple and delicious"
- "Quick weeknight dinner"
